Step 1: Fill Out Form Information

Ah, filling out the information- the hardest part. Actually, not with Rezit. You could send an old resume or just relay your basic information. Rezit takes your job titles and creates a description from analyzing job postings.

Step 2: Collaborate With Your Career Specialist

Once a order has been processed, Rezit will take your form information and make a beautiful resume. In this step they might contact you, just to make sure everything is perfect. We are a bit perfectionist.

Step 3: Receive and Succeed

Receive your resume with a format optimized to beat the Applicant Tracking Systems(ATS). Additionally, you get a cover letter or LinkedIn profile designed to hit all the key words needed for success.
